FAQ

Common Questions

Do you take custom cake orders?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for weddings, birthdays, and special events. Please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance for custom orders.

Are your products suitable for allergies?

We offer several gluten-free and vegan options. Please let us know about any allergies when ordering, and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide catering for events of all sizes. From breakfast meetings to wedding receptions, we've got you covered.

Can I pre-order items?

Pre-orders are welcome and encouraged, especially for weekends and holidays. Call us or use the contact form to place your order.
