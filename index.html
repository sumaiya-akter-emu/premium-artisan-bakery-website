Our Story

A Family Tradition of Excellence

What started as a small family bakery in 1998 has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution. Our founder, Marie Dubois, learned the art of French baking from her grandmother, and those cherished recipes remain at the heart of everything we create.

Every morning before dawn, our team of dedicated artisans begins crafting the breads and pastries that have earned us a loyal following. We believe in slow fermentation, premium ingredients, and the magic that happens when you truly love what you do.
