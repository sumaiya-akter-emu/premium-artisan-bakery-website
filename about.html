Our Story

A Legacy of Love & Flour

Since 1998, we've been baking with heart, honoring traditions while embracing innovation.

Where It All Began

In the spring of 1998, Marie Dubois opened the doors to a tiny bakery in the heart of our town. Armed with her grandmother's recipes and an unwavering commitment to quality, she set out to bring authentic French baking to the community.

The first years were challenging—long nights, early mornings, and countless batches of dough. But word spread about the exceptional croissants and the sourdough bread that rivaled anything in Paris. What started as a one-woman operation grew into a beloved institution.

Today, our team of passionate bakers continues Marie's legacy. We still rise before dawn, still use her original recipes, and still believe that every customer deserves bread made with love. Some things should never change.
